Welcome to the main OCG Website!

Who we are

Orange Cube Games ( referred to as OCG from now on ) is a group of friends that decided to make games for fun. Currently there are 4 members of OCG, and 3 members of the sub-divisional group OCS ( Orange Cube Studios, the people who make our websites look slightly more professional then the OCG itself, they also make music for our games and manage our social networks ).

OCG mainly focuses on bringing back the 20th century games back to modern life. We also specialize in 16&32-bit games, however we also do games-on-request.

Our Game Engine

We have two game engines to date; as listed below.

OE - Orange Engine

Focuses on quicker projects that are highly customizable; however it lacks in many key features ( such as animation and multiplayer compatibility ).

CO - Comedy Orange

A packet of dll's that are used in other game engines ( such as Unity ), and a couple of JAR's for Java games ( which we used to do, until some issues with the formats we use and how inefficient / demanding Java is.
